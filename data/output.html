Frosting Ingredients

No cake is complete without a sweet and buttery frosting. Here’s what you need:

Butter (1 ½ cups) Unsweetened cocoa (1 cup) Powdered sugar (5 cups) Milk (½ cup) Vanilla extract (2 teaspoons)

Just change the normal flour for gluten-free flour. As a tip, add boiling water before baking

Dairy-free

You can use either almond or soy milk, as well as a dairy-free frosting option

Step by Step

First

Add flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, salt and espresso powder to a large bowl and whisk

Second

Add milk, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla to the mixture and mix together. Then add boiling water

Third

Distribute the cake batter evenly between the prepared cake pans. Bake for 30-35 minutes
